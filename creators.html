Our Story

Ouch was founded by three developers with a shared passion for horror, storytelling, and player-driven design.

What began as a small collaborative project has evolved into a dedicated studio focused on delivering high-quality, immersive experiences within Fortnite Creative.

Our team brings experience from Roblox, Unreal Engine, and various independent projects, giving us a strong foundation in both technical development and creative direction.
